Lasagna Recipe

This is the recipe for lasagna below is items you will need.

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps for cooking Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the Ricotta mix
  4. Layer the lasagna with mixture
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
