Sushi

Below will be the recipe for homeade sushi

Ingredients

  • Japanese sushi rice
  • Konbu dried kelp
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Steps for making sushi

  1. Rinse Rice
  2. Combine Ingredients with rinsed sushi
  3. Boil rice covered over high heat
  4. Bring boiled rice to low heat simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Scrape rice into bowl remove konbu, stir in vinegar until no lumps remaining
